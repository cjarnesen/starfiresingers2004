LAUMC Starfire Singers
Southwest Tour
July 29, 2004



Hi folks!

We're having a great time so far! As always, you would be very proud of our Starfire Singers and the many ways they are spreading the message of God's love. We have done four shows so far, all to standing ovations and very good crowds. Other than a few problems with altitude, a cat allergy, and minor upset tummies and sore throats, everyone is in good health and great spirits. Colorado is beautiful, and a constant reminder of God's amazing creation.

As we pass the halfway point tonight, we are looking forward to moving into New Mexico and Arizona, then back into California and home. So all is well, LAUMC has amazing youth (and the chaperones aren't too shabby either), and God is blessing us in big ways and small ways as we journey together. Thanks for your prayers, your love, your support. We'll see you soon!

Dirk and Carol
